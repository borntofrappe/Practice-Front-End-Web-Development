Hello and welcome to live coverage from the Mexican Grand Prix...

We are here at the Autodromo Hermanos Rodriguez for Round 19 of the 2018 championship. Today could see Lewis Hamilton wrap up the title - starting third, one place ahead of Sebastian Vettel, the odds are in his favour...

Of course, Hamilton wrapped up his fourth world title at this race last year. Will history repeat itself? If it does, he will be just the third five-time world champion, after Juan Manuel Fangio and Michael Schumacher...
